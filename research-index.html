Intelligent Signal Processing (ISPRC)

The primary objective of the ISPRC is to develop and apply advanced algorithms and techniques for processing, analyzing, and interpreting signals. This includes improving existing methods and developing new ones to enhance the understanding of signal processing and its applications.

Advanced Image Processing (AIPRC)

Advanced Image Processing Cluster consists of high-performance servers, storage devices and softwares which are used for research and teaching purposes. The cluster is equipped with the latest image processing software and hardware components.

Advanced Manufacturing Technology (AMTRC)

Advanced Manufacturing Technology Cluster consists of high-performance systems, storage devices and softwares which are used for research and teaching purposes. The cluster is equipped with the latest software and hardware components.

Advanced Theoretical Computer Science (ATCSRC)

Advanced Theoretical Computer Science research cluster is established to promote research activities in Information Technology relevant to Human Computer Interaction, Information Management, Web Systems & Technologies and Networking

Alternate Energy (AERC)
