ALTERNATE ENERGY RESEARCH CLUSTER

 Home / ALTERNATE ENERGY RESEARCH CLUSTER

OBJECTIVES
       To stimulate research, innovation and sustainable value creation among the young minds in the field of alternate energy.
       To create a favourable forum for multidisciplinary collaboration for the enhancement of the renewable energy extraction and implementation
VISION

   To innovate ideas and invent products that makes the power sector smarter and the earth greener.


MISSION
       To promote research activities in the areas of new sources, smarter control and operation of alternate energy sources.
       To create awareness about the hazards of non-renewable energy, benefits alternate energy and necessity of energy savings.
